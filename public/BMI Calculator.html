Health Tips

General Health Tips

1.Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
2.Exercise regularly, aiming for at least 150 minutes of activity weekly.
3.Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.
4.Manage stress through relaxation techniques and social connections.
5.Stay up-to-date with regular health check-ups and vaccinations.
6.Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
7.Maintain a healthy weight with balanced eating and exercise.
8.Practice good hygiene and safety measures daily.
